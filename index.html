About the Branch

The IEEE Student Branch at UIT RGPV is a vibrant maker collective where creativity meets cutting-edge technology. We are a passionate community of innovators, engineers, developers, and creators dedicated to prototyping, hacking, publishing, and launching impactful projects. Our mission is to foster hands-on learning, encourage experimentation, and push the boundaries of technological innovation. We organize and host a wide range of activities, including immersive workshops focused on emerging technologies, AI hackathons (AI jams) that challenge participants to solve real-world problems, embedded systems projects that bridge hardware and software, and collaborative initiatives that span global borders. Whether you are developing IoT solutions, building intelligent robotics, exploring machine learning, or contributing to open-source projects, the IEEE Student Branch offers a supportive and inspiring environment. By connecting students with industry experts, researchers, and global innovators, we empower our members to learn, collaborate, and grow.
